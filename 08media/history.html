Production of Artwork

The historical study of the visual arts, being concerned with identifying, classifying, describing, evaluating, interpreting, and understanding the art products and historic development of the fields of painting, sculpture, architecture, the decorative arts, drawing, printmaking, photography, interior design, etc. Art historians analyze art objects to understand the meaning they had when they were created, and how they relate to the world around them. Art history is an interdisciplinary field that combines the study of history with the ability to interpret visual art.
