    • Painting Materials

    Painting materials can vary depending on what kind of painting you're into. There's acrylic painting, oil painting, and watercolor painting. Each need different materials for the medium being used.


    Oil Painting Materials

    1. Canvas
    2. Oil Paints
    3. Paint Brushes
    4. Easel
    5. Palette
    6. Palette Knives

    Watercolor Painting Materials

    1. Watercolor Paper or Canvas
    2. Watercolor Paint
    3. Palette
    4. Paint Brushes
    5. Painter's Tape/Masking Tape
    6. Cup, Jar, or anything to hold Water
    7. Masking Fluid and Eraser
    8. Paper Towels
    9. Squirt Bottle

    Drawing Materials

    1. Graphite Pencils
    2. Paper
    3. Rulers
    4. Pencil Sharpener
    5. Erasers
    6. Charcoal
    7. Smudgers

    Ceramics Materials

    1. Clay
    2. Potter's Wheel (optional depending on your scultpure)
    3. Cup for Water
    4. Exacto Knife
    5. Sponges
    6. Wire

    Calligraphy Materials

    1. Paper
    2. Calligraphy Pens
    3. Ink
    4. Ruler
    5. Pencils
    6. Eraser
    7. Pens
